How to Play

Welcome to Orbital Defense!

  • Use LEFT/RIGHT arrow keys or A/D to rotate your defense platform
  • Press SPACE or the fire button to shoot at incoming enemies
  • Press 1, 2, 3 or the weapon buttons to switch between weapons
  • Different weapons are effective against different enemy types
  • Shooting consumes energy - watch your energy level!
  • Survive all waves to complete the mission
