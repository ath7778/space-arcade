How to Play

Surf across the sun's surface on your quantum board, performing tricks and avoiding danger.

  • Left/Right Arrow Keys: Move your surfer
  • Up Arrow: Jump off a solar flare
  • Down Arrow: Drop down or dive
  • Space Bar: Perform aerial tricks (only when airborne)
  • Avoid: Solar storms and excessive heat

Pro Tip: Linking multiple tricks creates combo multipliers!

Warning: Staying on a flare too long will overheat your board!
