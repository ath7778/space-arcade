Your space station's reactor core is overheating and multiple systems are failing!

How to Play

Welcome to Space Station Meltdown!

  • Use WASD or arrow keys to navigate through the station
  • Press SPACE or the repair button to fix broken systems
  • Find and repair all 5 critical systems before your oxygen runs out
  • Oxygen levels drop faster the longer systems remain damaged
  • Some doors may be locked - find the keycard to access them
