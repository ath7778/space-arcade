How to Play

Welcome to Cosmic Drift!

  • Use WASD or arrow keys to fire thrusters in different directions
  • Hold SPACE or the boost button for a temporary speed boost
  • Collect blue crystals to increase your score
  • Avoid red obstacles and asteroid fields
  • Reach the green portal to complete each level
  • Thrusters consume fuel - use them wisely!
