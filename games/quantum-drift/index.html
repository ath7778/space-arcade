How to Play

Navigate your quantum ship through a procedurally generated wormhole tunnel.

  • Left/Right Arrow Keys: Steer your ship
  • Space: Activate quantum boost
  • Avoid: Tunnel walls and quantum anomalies
  • Collect: Energy particles to refill your boost meter

The further you travel, the faster the tunnel becomes and the more points you earn!
