How to Play Asteroid Belt Dogfight

  • Use WASD or arrow keys to control your spacecraft
  • Left-click or Space to fire your selected weapon
  • Numbers 1, 2, 3 to switch between weapons
  • Destroy enemy fighters to earn points
  • Avoid asteroids and enemy fire
  • Collect power-ups to recharge ammo and health

Weapons

  • Laser (1): Rapid fire, low damage
  • Missile (2): Slow fire rate, high damage, area effect
  • Shield (3): Temporary invulnerability, consumes energy

Controls

  • W/Up Arrow: Thrust forward
  • A/Left Arrow: Rotate left
  • D/Right Arrow: Rotate right
  • S/Down Arrow: Brake
  • Space/Left Click: Fire weapon
  • ESC: Pause game
