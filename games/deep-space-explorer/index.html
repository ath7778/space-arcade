How to Play Deep Space Explorer

  • Use arrow keys to navigate your spacecraft through deep space
  • Scan areas to discover celestial bodies and phenomena
  • Each discovery increases your score
  • Manage your fuel wisely - scanning and boosting consume fuel
  • Refuel by passing close to certain celestial objects
  • The expedition ends when you run out of fuel

Controls

  • Arrow Keys: Navigate your spacecraft
  • Space: Activate scanner
  • Shift: Boost speed (uses more fuel)
  • ESC: Pause the game
