How to Play

  • Type the words on the asteroids to destroy them
  • Each successful hit earns you points
  • If an asteroid hits your ship, you lose a life
  • Game ends when you run out of lives
  • Speed increases with each level

Controls

  • Type words in the input field to target asteroids
  • Press ESC to pause the game
  • Press Enter to confirm your input
